# Digitization Center

A place to get help in the digital age.

A record store for the digital age.

A local Spotify/Netflix.

A local exchange location.

A digital donation center?
  - Dependent upon the location.
  - Is there adequate value in the repair/resale of this product?
  - Prove minimum repair/resale value.

A physical establishment built by open source.

## Principles

* Open-source everything - guides, hardware parts lists, and configurations are open.
* Pay by the hour/service.
* "Teach a man to fish" philosophy. A need to share knowledge.
* Plug in a technology.

## Problems

* Local tech-based establishments don't focus on the needs of the consumer.

## Services

* Digitize a book.
* Analog to Cloud
  - CD/Cassette/Vinyl/Audio to Cloud (for multiple formats)
  - sVHS/VHS/DVD/Video to Cloud (for multiple formats)
* Analog to Digital
* Digital to Digital
* Video Game Backup
* Renting/Purchasing/Reselling/Storage
* Repair
* Advice
* Up-front service plans
* Record an exchange of goods (Craigslist/Letgo/eBay verification)
* Determine best backup procedure based on data type and size (walk someone through backing up their data)
  - Video
  - Audio
  - Coursework
* Verification step, always
* Digitize an album on expensive equipment.
* Save the Vinyl
  - How to clean record sleeves
  - How to clean records
  - Digitize it.

## Supported Formats

- Disc (CD/DVD/Blu-ray)
- SD
- CD
- DVD
- Floppy (3.5/5.25/8)

## Points at which someone should pay

* Purchasing of goods
* Purchasing of services - price per hour of human time, of utilities cost, of resource cost.
  - kwh used
  - human hours exhausted (first 15/minutes, free)
  - gph used
