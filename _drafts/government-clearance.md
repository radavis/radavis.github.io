# Government Clearance

## Process

1. Complete forms, including a [questionnaire](https://www.opm.gov/Forms/pdf_fill/SF86.pdf).
2. A Background Investigation is conducted.
  - National Agency Record Checks
  - Fingerprint Checks
  - Investigation of key events and contacts from your history
3. Face-to-face interview with Department of State [DS] Investigator.
