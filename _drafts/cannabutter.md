## Soak and Blanch (optional)

Removes bitter tasting chlorophyll.

* soak in distilled water for 1 to 2 days (24 to 48 hours).
* change the water every 12 hours.
* blanch: boil for 5 minutes, ice water for 1 minute


## Decarb

![Decarboxylation Graph](/assets/images/decarboxylation-graph.jpg)

using dry herb...

* spread onto baking sheet
* cover with aluminum foil
* bake @ 252F for 27 minutes (or 240F for 40 minutes)


## Break Down Plant Cellulose w/ Alcohol

* spray sheet of decarbed herb with grain alcohol (everclear or similar)
* rest for 15 minutes


## Butter + Herb, Apply Heat

### Ingredients

* 2 cups water
* 1 oz (28g) ground, decarbed herb to 1 lb (454g) butter

### Instructions

1. add water and butter to pot or slow cooker, wait until melted
2. add herb, stir
3. simmer @ 200F for 3 hours, or low setting on slow cooker for 6 hrs
4. wait until cool, strain with cheesecloth and mesh strainer
5. chill in refrigerator overnight, cannabutter and water will separate
6. break up cannabutter with fork and store in plastic wrap

## Green Dragon

* submerge in grain alcohol
* shake
* wait 8-12 hours
