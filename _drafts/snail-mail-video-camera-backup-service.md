# Managing other's personal files

## Options

* Host on cloud service, such as Dropbox, Mega, or Drive
  - Manage the tradeoff of usernames/accounts
  - Utilize existing mobile apps
  - Funnel customers to other services? This seems wrong.
* Host on AWS/Heroku/Digital Ocean
  - Create links to other common services
  - Easier to scale
  - More configurable/more complex

## Features

* Video camera backup solution.
* Mail in old device or old media.
  - w/ power cable & data cable, discount provided.
  - Envelope and shipping provided.
* Cloud Storage (1 year limited service)
* Download Backup to Personal Device
* Export to other services (YouTube, Drive, etc)

## Internal Problems to Solve

* Inventory
* Supported Devices
  - Type in Make & Model -> (Supported, Unsupported)
  - Email customers when device is moved from unsupported to supported
* Intake
  - Is the incoming delivery as described?
  - Enter into processing queue.
* Processing Queue
  - Incoming shipping
  - Received
  - In queue
  - Recording (streaming), Incoming video channel
  - Email download link, export to dropbox, drive, mega, etc
* Device to device transfer
  - Where do you want your data to end up?
  - Best option based on input.
