# Checklists for tracking equipment and the maintenance of that equipment

## Questions

Q: Does the hardware in every office work correctly? No? How do we fix this?
A: Daily/Weekly/Monthly checks.
  * Audio
  * Video
  * Ethernet
  * Wireless

Q: What should this office have?
A: Checklist:
  * [ ] TV
  * [ ] Projector
  * [ ] Remotes (Where should they go?)
  * [ ] Phone
  * [ ] Cables (HDMI, Ethernet)
  * [ ] Adapters

Once marking the checklist complete, the technician is asked to verify that items
are missing from the room.


## Software Idea: Build a issue reporting system where the office blueprint is the central focus.

The Simple Problem: Reporting, documenting, and fixing office hardware issues across different technologies is not easily managed.

Features:
  * Photos and sub-photos
    - Upload a photo/blueprint
    - Self-referential photos/blueprints
  * Photo rectangle/area identification (building, rooms, walls, desk/table, shelf)
  * CRUD Building Issues
    - Report an issue: Select a room/area,
    - View list of issues (by building, by type, by date)

We should listen to the problems of our customers.
  * Workbarbarians, first
  * Admin users

Tablet Questionnaire for a room or area:

How is this room? Are there any issues in this area that need to be addressed?
  * Is it clean? Y/n -> Direct issue to phone number, email, sms.
    - How bad is it? -> The surfaces could use a wipe-down./It could use some straightening up./Bring a mop.
  * WiFi trouble/Phone issues/TV or Projector problems?  
    - Severity -> Annoying, Frustrating, Not a huge deal.
  * Does it need to be resupplied?
    - Paper
    - Ink
    - Coffee
  * Take photo/video of reporter
  * Admin panel for managing the questions on display at each terminal
