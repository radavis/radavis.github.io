I once worked for an employer who claimed to be liberal, and then proceeded to
tell me a story about how he fired a guy who couldn't read color-codes on
resistors fast enough. The employee that was fired was colorblind. The employer
was aware of this disability.

It's possible that no one was up to my employer's standards. I decided to cut
ties from this particular mail-order Electronics Repair shop when I was told
that I needed to have a "fire lit under my ass." Maybe I'm slow. Maybe it takes
me a few tries before I figure something out. I'm fine with this "disability"
not being officially diagnosed, prescribed, and observed.

Since my career in Electronics Repair and Calibration, I've learned how to
create experiences on the web. The majority of work comes in from the digitization
of paper forms and processes. But, that's the future, isn't it!?
