**Beginner's Mind**

Approach the situations in your life without judging them to be good or bad, positive or negative. Enter every situation and every relationship as you were seeing it for the first time.

**The problem with Judgement**

- Judgements can trigger overwhelming emotions.
- Judgements can lead to disappointment and suffering.
- Judgements can prevent you from being truly mindful in the present moment.

**Grounding**

The process of grounding involves becoming mindful of your physical senses in this moment. What do you see, hear, smell, touch, and taste?
