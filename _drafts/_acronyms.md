According to the Modern Language Association (MLA) Handbook, "A term must be
fully written the first time it is used, thereafter just the acronym is used,"
in a document, essay, or publication.

Likewise, when giving a presentation, use the full term and the acronym. Then,
you may use the acronym throughout your presentation.

When using an acronym in HTML documents, the `<abbr>` element should be used to
define the acronym or initialism.

https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/abbr
