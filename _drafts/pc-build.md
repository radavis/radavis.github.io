## Thoughts

* Will an ATX power supply fit in the Systemax 731i-300B case? __Yes__
* How much space will the motherboard take up? __Case can handle mATX 9.6"x9.6"__
* How much space will the graphics card take up? __1.5"x4.4"x8.7"__
* Is 700W enough for CrossfireX? __Go with 800W to be safe__
* Power supply may go out of stock at Microcenter. Order on [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com/Coolmax-ZU-800B-CrossFire-Certified-Modular/dp/B007WLRGY8) before Thursday.

## Case/MB/GFX + Crossfire conflict

### Problems

* Not enough expansion slots on Systemax 731i Case (4)
* PCIe slots at positions 1 and 4 of Gigabyte GA-H97M-D3H MB
* Video card must be less than 9" in length

### Solutions

* New case w/ > 4 slots
* Use Kingwin case
* Different MB with PCIe slots at 1 and 3
