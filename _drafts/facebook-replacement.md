Facebook is a billon-dollar company that has monetized our opinions and preferences.

You pay nothing for their service. It's a place for anyone to publish their thoughts
and photos for free. In that respect, it's pretty great.

However, this "free" Internet product makes its payroll through our personal data.
  1. You input your personal experiences to share with friends and family.
  2. This information is stored and indexed.
  3. This information is used to select advertisements to show to you.
  4. This information is sold to 3rd parties.

Not only has the sale of your personal data made their payroll, **it's made their
CEO one of the top 10 richest people alive**[*][forbes-billionaires].
