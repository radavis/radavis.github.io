# Buying a used vehicle in Maryland

## Seller

* _Certificate of Title_
  - fill out 'Assignment of Ownership' on the Title w/ buyer and seller's information
  - record odometer mileage
  - record purchase price and date of sale
  - sign
* _Bill of Sale_ is not necessary for vehicles is over 7 years old
* _Notice of Security Interest Filing_ proving the vehicle has been paid for

## Buyer

Bring the following documents to a MVA office.

* _Certificate of Title_
* _Notice of Security Interest Filing_ or letter from financial institution
* Pay excise tax
* Inspection or Temporary Registration
* Insurance
* Application for Title and Registration

## Questions

* Is the vehicle inspection valid? When does it expire?
* Can you provide proof that the vehicle has been paid off? (e.g. - Can you provide
  a Notice of Security Interest Filing?)
