# Problems that I have observed

Remote pairing audio quality is shit.
  - Provide pairs with high quality headsets.
  - Station them as property of the Fearless office.
  - Allow staff working at the office to check them out.

Audio quality during ceremonies is shit.
  - Provide an ethernet connection in all conference rooms.
  - Allow staff to check out an appropriate microphone.
  - Allow staff to check out an ethernet adapter and cable.

Technology Locker: a place to check out tools, temporarily.
  - ethernet adapters
  - usb hubs
  - external disks
  - cdrom/dvd drives

Solve the technology problem for everyone, and not just ourselves.
