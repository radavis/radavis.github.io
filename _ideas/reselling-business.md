# Reselling Business

## Intake

* Record information about the device.
  - Name/Model
  - Manufacturer
  - Release Date
  - Purchase Date
  - Purchase Price
* Take photographs
* Assess condition
  - New or used?
  - Working or for parts/repair?
* Package, label, and store
  - Type: box, padded envelope, letter
  - Package dimensions
  - Weight
