# App Idea

A visual representation of a foreign currency, in your daily currency.

## A Mapping

1) Select your home country, allow the application to discover your current location.

2) Select your remote country.

3) The price of the following is displayed...
  - a pack of gum
  - a bottle of water
  - a soda (can, bottle)
  - a pack of cigarettes
  - a candy bar
  - a shot of liquor
  - a cab ride (per mile/km)
  - a sandwich
  - a beer
  - a bus or subway ride

## Key

* base conversion (USD)
